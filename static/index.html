Why adversarial reasoning works

The legal system discovered this centuries ago: truth is more reliable when every claim must survive a skilled challenge. Science calls it peer review. Philosophy calls it dialectics. We've never applied it to AI — until now.

The problem with regular AI answers

Every AI response is a closing argument with no opposing counsel. The model optimizes for sounding correct, not being correct. There is no mechanism that forces it to confront what it might be wrong about.

What the synthesis gives you

The synthesis isn't a compromise. It's what a genuinely honest expert would say after hearing both sides — including which side has the stronger case and what specific evidence would change the verdict.

Adversary — adversarial reasoning for every question. The FOR case never represents Adversary's views. Neither does the AGAINST case. Only the synthesis does.
